The main idea of our project

The project "Zelenskiy Ukraine NFT Collection" is created during the war in Ukraine.

Every person of our team saw the result of the russion aggression with our own eyes. This motivated us to create a charitable project to help the Ukrainian army.

The goal of our project is to spread the key facts about Russia’s invasion of Ukraine all over the world through of the NFT collection.

This will not only allow the proceeds to be used to help the Ukrainian army, which could save thousands of lives, but also to convey the truth to the people. The truth and values for which Ukrainians have to die every day.
